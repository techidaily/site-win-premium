Issue 1:  
Step 1:  
 Properties for “Text” configuration: File: CSV files to Detect:  
 The 1st one “Comma separated” is checked.  
Step 2:  
 Customize: CSV:  
 Move up “Tab separated” to the top.  
Step 3:  
 Properties for “Text” configuration: File: CSV files to Detect:  
 \*\* Now “Tab separated” is checked.  
—————  
 Issue 2:  
 When a CSV file is open, and if the columns are wide, the current position could be beyond the right edge instead of in the visible area.  
 I guess this is because the column expanding happens after the position jumping.  
By the way,  
 I think the “Jump to the first invalid character” option also needs an accelerator key.
